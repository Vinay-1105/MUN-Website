What is VIT-B MUN?

Welcome to MUN-VITB! As the premier Model United Nations (MUN) club at Vellore Institute of Technology, Bhopal, we are dedicated to fostering global citizenship, critical thinking, and diplomacy among our members. Through our dynamic programs and activities, we provide students with unique opportunities to engage with international issues and develop leadership skills that help make a positive impact in their communities.

Community

  • UNSC

    Ensures global peace and security through sanctions, peacekeeping missions, and military action. Acts as a unified force to address conflicts and promote stability worldwide.
    Agenda:
    Situation in the Middle East, including the Israel - Palestine conflict.

  • AIPPM

    A platform for Indian political parties to discuss and deliberate on national issues. Facilitates decision-making on key policies for the country's development and governance.
    Agenda:
    Reviving statehood for both Jammu and Kashmir and Ladakh, including tribal status for Ladakh under the 6th schedule of the Indian constitution.

  • UNHRC

    Promotes and safeguard global human rights worldwide through investigations and advocacy.
    Agenda:
    Protecting rights of non-combatant civilians in war zones (Russia-Ukraine, Israel-Palestine, etc.)

About us

Founded in 2014, MUN-VITB stands as a beacon of leadership and innovation in the realm of Model United Nations. Our club empowers students to delve into critical global challenges, engage in dynamic debates, and refine their skills in diplomacy and public speaking. With a legacy of exceptional performance, we take pride in fostering a community of changemakers who consistently excel at prestigious conferences and competitions, earning widespread recognition and privilege.
